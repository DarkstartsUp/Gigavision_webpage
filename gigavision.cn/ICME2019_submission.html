Submission Guidelines

The workshop will consist of 2-3 invited talks given by leading researchers in gigapixel imaging and computer vision. We invite submission of original high-quality contributions. However, it is also welcomed to submit relevant work that has been recently published, is in progress, or is to be presented other venues including the ICME main conference. Accepted submissions will be invited to present either as talks, or posters at the workshop. We will be awarding a best paper award.

Authors are invited to submit a full paper (two-column format, no longer than 6 pages including all text, figures, and references). Submissions should follow the ICME format . To submit your paper, go through the paper submission process at the ICME website to upload the pdf of your paper. Papers must be submitted under the track for 1st Workshop of Computer Vision in Gigapixel Videography (Gigavision). Only electronic submissions will be accepted. Review process will be double blind.

Please register an account if you have any interest in our workshop and submit your challenge mode here!
