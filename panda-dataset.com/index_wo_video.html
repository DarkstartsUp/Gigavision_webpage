PANDA Dataset Introduction

PANDA is the first gigapixel-level human-centric video dataset, for large-scale, long-term, and multi-object visual analysis. The videos in PANDA were captured by a gigapixel camera and cover real-world scenes with both wide field-of-view (~1 km^2 area) and high resolution details (~gigapixel-level/frame). The scenes may contain 4k head counts with over 100× scale variation. PANDA provides enriched and hierarchical ground-truth annotations, including 10,218.4k bounding boxes, 111.8k fine-grained attribute labels, 8.4k trajectories, 1.5k groups and 2.9k interactions.

If you use our dataset, please cite the following paper:

Multiscale gigapixel video: A cross resolution image matching and warping approach
Xiaoyun Yuan, Lu Fang, Qionghai Dai, David J Brady, Yebin Liu. ICCP, 2017
